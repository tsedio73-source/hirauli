Privacy Policy

Introduction

Hirauli and Kutubnagar are two combined villages and this portal helps people to update news related to the village. People of the village can upload photos or videos on this portal. The history of the village is also written on this web page. Here people can log in and post in the web gallery section, and people can like and comment on posts. Those people who do not know the location of the village can find it easily through the website. This website is published on the domain https://tsedio73-source.github.io/hirauli/. The Hirauli website saves data on Cloudinary and Firebase DB. First it was made by Ashish Rajput in November 2025. AI is also used on this website for earlier updates. This website is updated within 3 to 4 months.

What is important

This website is for educational purposes only. No document here is authorized by the Government. If you are visiting these pages for any revenue office work, then you are going wrong. You should contact any Government portal for work related to legal documents.

This website provides options where you can upload one post with a title or multiple posts without a title. You can like, share, comment, and download pictures or videos while logged in with your Google account. But you can't delete anything. This feature is only available to the admin of the website. Still, you can send feedback and say to the admin "Please delete any specific type of pictures or videos" because that is the privacy rule of Firebase.

How to use

Please provide legal media and text on this website, and only content related to these villages, not any other places. If you are a person from these villages, you should upload photos or videos on this portal and let everyone know that your village has a digital portal. One more thing: if you are a member of the village, you should read the history of the village at least one time, which is available on this website in the section Information.

If you have any new idea related to changes on the web pages, you can send feedback which is available in the help section.

Keeping your information secure

This website stores its data on Firebase and Cloudinary, one for text and one for pictures and clips. User authentication is handled by Firebase. Any user can't see other users' information except the media uploaded by users. Your Google account remains totally safe after login. Any phishing is not allowed on these web pages because this is a legal website, but it should not be used for official documents.
